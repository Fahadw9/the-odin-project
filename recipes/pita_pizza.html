Barbecue Chicken Pita Pizza

Description:

Running low on the pizza supplies but still hungry for pizza? Well, what if i told you that you can make a pizza at home without a baking oven or pizza dough. Yes, its possible to make a pizza with pita bread (shawarma bread), within minutes. The delicious taste of chicken barbecue pizza can be made replicated through making the pita pizza

Ingredients:

  • Flat Pita Bread
  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • ½ cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Cilantro or olives for toppings

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. In a small bowl, mix chicken and ¼ cup barbecue until combined. Set aside.
  3. Spread 2 tablespoons of barbecue sauce onto pita. Top with a thin layer of Mozzarella cheese, followed by the barbecue sauce, more cheese, or toppings of your choice.
  4. Place each pita on a plate. Heat in oven for 3-5 minutes. Remove from oven and cut into slices.
