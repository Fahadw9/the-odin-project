Paratha Roll

Description

Seekh Kababs are a staple in our kitchen. They are so easy to make and enjoy fresh, but they are also the perfect candidates to turn an already delicious kabab into a spectacular meal! We love having seekh kabab rolls to make into a curry. But these seekh kabab rolls are always a winner around here!

Ingredients

  • Kebab
  • Paratha
  • Ketchup/Chilli Garlic/Mayonnaise according to your taste
  • Onion according to your taste
  • Lettuce - 2 Peices
  • Handful of love

Steps

  1. Heat the fresh or frozen parathas (flakey flatbread).
  2. Add seekh kabab
  3. Spread your desired amount of the chosen sauces
  4. Cut the onion into small peices and spread it evenly
  5. Add the peices of luttuce
  6. Roll the paratha as a roll and serve them with loads of love.
